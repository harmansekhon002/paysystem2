There are dozens of time trackers and shift planners, but most ignore the realities of student visas. We tested popular options to see which ones actually help international students working abroad.

## What to look for in a student shift app

- Visa‑aware work hour tracking (weekly or fortnightly caps)
- Penalty rate calculations for weekends and public holidays
- Simple budgeting and savings goals
- Offline support for patchy campus Wi‑Fi
- Privacy: data should stay yours

## Why general time trackers fall short

Most popular tools are built for agencies or freelancers. They log time but do not warn you when you approach a visa cap, and they do not understand penalty rates. That means you still have to do the math and compliance tracking yourself.
